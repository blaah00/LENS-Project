Step 1
Select training type
LENS uses differentiated evaluation logic for each training type. Select the category that best describes this training program.

Technical / Functional

Job-specific skills, tools, and measurable output improvement. Evaluated using knowledge tests and KPI data.

Behavioral

Communication, collaboration, conflict resolution, and accountability. Evaluated through multi-rater perception.

Leadership

Decision-making, delegation, and team impact. Includes reportee feedback as a primary measurement source.

Organizational

Policy adherence, ownership, adaptability, and cultural alignment. Evaluated by manager and self-assessment.

⚡

Fast-track eligible

Pre-assessment score is 9 or above. Based on LENS logic, this employee may not require this training program. Review with the relevant manager before proceeding.

Step 2
Pre-assessment (Baseline)
Enter scores before training begins. All scores are out of 10. This establishes the baseline against which all improvement is measured.
Step 3
Post-assessment (After training)
Enter scores after training is completed. Use the same sources as the pre-assessment to ensure comparability.
Step 4
On-the-job KPI measurement
Enter business performance metrics before and after training. This validates whether learning translated into actual job impact.
